Welcome to JinendraVani.in

JinendraVani.in was founded in 2025 by Shivanshu Jain from Amritsar, a city known for its spiritual heritage.

This platform was created to preserve and share the rich culture, devotion, and teachings of Jain Dharma with the world.

1. What We Offer:

  • Aarti: Devotional songs for the Tirthankars
  • Pooja: Rituals and methods for worship
  • Bhajan: Soulful Jain devotional songs
  • Chalisa: Powerful 40-verse praises
  • Mantra: Sacred Jain mantras like Navkar Mantra
  • Jain History: Teachings and stories of Tirthankars and saints
  • Festivals & Vrats: Information on Jain festivals and spiritual fasting and much more...

Our Vision:

To inspire spiritual growth and devotion by spreading Jain values and teachings in a simple and digital format.

Our Founder:

Shivanshu Jain is passionate about Jain philosophy and digital seva (service). Through this initiative, he aims to bring the message of Jinendra to every home and heart.

Why "JinendraVani"?

"Jinendra" means the conqueror of inner enemies — our Tirthankars. "Vani" means voice. Thus, JinendraVani means the voice and teachings of the Enlightened Ones.
